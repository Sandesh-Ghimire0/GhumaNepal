About Nepal

Nepal, officially the Federal Democratic Republic of Nepal, is a landlocked country located in South Asia. It is known for its diverse geography, rich cultural heritage, and friendly peopl. Nepal is home to the majestic Himalayas, including the world's highest peak, Mount Everest. The country features a variety of landscapes, from lowland plains to rugged mountains and lush valleys. The cultural diversity of Nepal is reflected in its festivals, art, music, and traditions. The people of Nepal celebrate various festivals throughout the year, showcasing their vibrant cultural heritage. >Nepal is a popular destination for trekking and mountaineering enthusiasts. The capital city, Kathmandu, is known for its historic temples and vibrant street life. The country offers breathtaking views and opportunities for adventure.
